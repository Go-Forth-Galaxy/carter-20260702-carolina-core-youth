Recreation

Thank you to all the volunteer coaches that make this league possible! Our CCFC Recreation program focuses on the development of our younger players while making it a fun and exciting experience — the first building block of the player pathway, with leagues in Kernersville & High Point.

Locations

Season Dates

Take the Next Step

Juniors

The Juniors program develops U7–U10 players and prepares them to enter the Youth Club — the bridge between Recreation and competitive Classic soccer. U7–U10, Girls & Boys, Fall 2026 – Spring 2027.

Compete

Classic

U11–U19 competitive players compete in the tiered levels of NCYSA Classic. Our top U10–U12 teams compete alongside our Pre-GA & Pre-MLS teams; our U13+ top teams compete in MLS NEXT, Girls Academy Aspire, and the National League. U11–U19, Girls & Boys, Fall 2026 – Spring 2027.

Top Players

Pre-GA & Pre-MLS

Our top U10–U12 players are invited to the Pre-GA & Pre-MLS teams. These teams compete against the other top teams in North Carolina and feed directly into our Girls Academy and MLS NEXT programs.

Elite Girls Pathway

Girls Academy

CCFC Youth is proud to offer competitive pathways for our girls through the Girls Academy — a high-intensity competitive platform that challenges players to develop both technically and tactically while preparing them for collegiate opportunities. We also field GA Aspire teams as the next tier of the girls competitive pathway.

Elite Boys Pathway

MLS NEXT

CCFC Youth offers competitive pathways for our boys through MLS NEXT Homegrown, MLS NEXT Academy Division, and NCYSA. Our top boys teams compete in MLS NEXT — a high-intensity platform preparing players technically and tactically for collegiate opportunities. MLS NEXT Pro: Carolina Core FC joined MLS NEXT Pro for the 2024 season as the second independent, non-MLS affiliated club in the league.

Who We Are

Mission & Guiding Principles

CCFC Youth Mission

Our mission is to create a dynamic and inclusive soccer club that prioritizes excellence, a strong sense of community, and holistic development — empowering players to grow both on and off the field, and instilling values that inspire success in every aspect of life.

Guiding Principles

Excellence

We uphold a standard of excellence in communication, operations, coaching, programs, and facilities.

Growth and Innovation

We continually expand the opportunities, experiences and pathways for our players, from recreational beginnings to elite opportunities.

Community and Inclusivity

We cultivate connections among players, families, and coaches — a soccer family committed to programs for girls and boys of all levels.

Character Development

We reinforce teamwork, resilience, dedication and constant improvement, shaping players into confident, capable people on and off the field.

FAQs

How do I register on PlayMetrics?

Head to our Register section to create your PlayMetrics account, verify your email, add each player, and complete registration. Contact support@playmetrics.com for account help.

Refund Policy

The initial payment securing a roster spot is non-refundable. Refund requests must be submitted in writing and are reviewed by the Board of Directors Financial Committee.

Payment Delinquency

Installments are due the 15th of the month. If auto-draft fails, submit payment by the 30th. Players may be suspended from all privileges if payment is 15+ days late.

Register

All Carolina Core FC Youth programs register through our PlayMetrics portal. Follow these steps to secure your player's spot for the 2026-2027 season — Recreation, Juniors, Classic, Girls Academy, and MLS NEXT.

How to Register

  1. Create a free PlayMetrics account and verify your email.
  2. Add each player to your household profile (name, birth year, and photo).
  3. Use the Age Group Shift chart to confirm your player's age group.
  4. Choose your program and pay the registration fee (installment plans available).
  5. Complete the required waivers, code of conduct, and media release.

What You'll Need

  • Player's legal name and date of birth
  • A recent head-and-shoulders photo of each player
  • Payment method (fees and installment options shown at checkout)
  • For competitive teams: your coach's or director's roster confirmation

Join Our Staff

Coaching Application

Passionate about developing young players and people of character? Carolina Core FC Youth is always looking for dedicated coaches across every level — from first-touch Recreation to our elite MLS NEXT and Girls Academy programs.

What We Look For

  • A commitment to player development and our guiding principles
  • Relevant coaching licenses (or a willingness to earn them — we support Coaching Education)
  • Reliability, strong communication, and a positive, professional presence
  • A cleared background check (required for all coaches)

How to Apply

  1. Email your resume and coaching background to our Coaching Education Director.
  2. Include the age groups and programs you're interested in.
  3. We'll follow up to discuss openings, licensing, and next steps.
Send applications to Peter Broadley, Coaching Education Director — info@carolinacoreyouth.com. Volunteer Recreation coaches are always welcome; no prior experience required — we'll train and support you.
Officiate the Game

Refereeing Application

Referees keep our game safe, fair, and fun. Carolina Core FC Youth welcomes new and experienced officials for Recreation and competitive matches across Kernersville and High Point — a great paid opportunity for players aged 13+, parents, and community members.

Getting Started

  • Complete the NCYSA / US Soccer Grassroots referee certification course.
  • Referees must be at least 13 years old to officiate youth matches.
  • Bring a whistle and a positive, impartial attitude — we'll help with the rest.
  • New officials are mentored by our experienced referee crew.

How to Apply

Email your interest, age, and any existing certifications and we'll connect you with the certification schedule and available match assignments.

To sign up or ask questions, email info@carolinacoreyouth.com with the subject line "Refereeing".
