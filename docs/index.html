Choose Your Program

Every player has a place at Carolina Core FC Youth. Find the level that fits your child today — and the pathway that grows with them.

Recreational

Where every player starts. Fun-first, low-pressure soccer that builds coordination, friendships, and a love for the game close to home.

Ages 3–14

Academy

The bridge between rec and competitive. Small-group training focused on technical foundations, first touch, and 1v1 confidence.

Ages 5–10

Select / Competitive

Travel teams competing in NCYSA leagues and regional tournaments, developing serious players through year-round, structured training.

Ages 8–18

Core IDP

Individual Development Program — supplemental, position-specific training and camps built to accelerate players outside team sessions.

All Ages

Camps & Clinics

Break camps, holiday clinics, and skills intensives open to the community — a great first step into the Carolina Core family.
